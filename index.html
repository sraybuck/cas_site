Preparing you for every step of the process

Overview

College Admissions Services is a comprehensive coaching service where your student will meet with our team of college coaches and receive guidance and expertise throughout the college applications and admissions process.

Our coaches will guide students through:

  • Identifying dream, target, and safety schools
  • Writing college essays
  • Completing college resumes
  • Applying to schools
  • Applying for financial aid and scholarships
  • Preparing for interviews
  • Submitting honors applications
  • Selecting a college

After consulting with the parent and student, our college coach will identify goals and expectations for the student. When a plan has been agreed upon, the student and coach will meet regularly to achieve these set goals. Our goal at College Admissions Services is to help your student achieve their goals and reach the highest level of achievement possible for your student.

Coaching Services

We offer the following services when meeting with students:

  • Student advising and resume prep beginning as early as 8th grade
  • Selecting and setting up college visits
  • Determine appropriate stretch, target, and safety schools
  • Guidance while completing CommonApp, Coalition Application, and individual university applications
  • Register and send scores for SAT/ACT
  • Assisting in the creation and editing of ZeeMee accounts
  • FAFSA application assistance and review
  • Bright Futures application assistance
  • Honors applications and essays review
  • Scholarship applications and essays review
  • Interview prep
  • College selection guidance - finding the right fit for your student
  • Assisting students with Naviance
  • Assisting and advising students in transition to college
  • Preparing for orientation and completing housing applications
