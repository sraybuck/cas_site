College Admissions Services LLC

Preparing you for every step of the process

Overview

College Admissions Services is a comprehensive coaching service where your student will meet with our team of college coaches and receive guidance and expertise throughout the college applications and admissions process.

Our coaches will guide students through:

  • Identifying dream, target, and safety schools
  • Completing college resumes
  • Writing college essays
  • Applying to schools
  • Applying for financial aid and scholarships
  • Preparing for interviews
  • Submitting honors applications
  • Selecting a college

After consulting with the parent and student, our college coach will identify goals and expectations for the student. When a plan has been agreed upon, the student and coach will meet regularly to achieve these set goals. Our goal at College Admissions Services is to help your student achieve their goals and reach the highest level of achievement possible for your student.

About Us

College Admissions Services, LLC was created in the spring of 2010 by Darla Neal. Darla’s corporate experience was a natural transition to comprehending the college admissions process. She quickly built a client base and has placed over 500 students to major universities. Our mission is to work with students and parents within St. Johns and Duval counties to identify schools that match the student's academic and social interests and then guide both students and parents through the entire application process: resume, essay, application and scholarships. Darla is a member of Southern Association for College Admission Counseling and Independent Educational Consultants Association, nationally recognized organizations for college counseling.


Darla Neal

Darla Neal attended the University of Texas and is a graduate of the University of Florida with a B.S. in Business Administration and Marketing. She has worked for Merck & Co. and Johnson & Johnson in sales, training, physician education and management. She was also a director with The American Heart Association. Darla has been actively involved in her community as a board member and leader of the Ponte Vedra Women’s Civic Alliance, IB Booster Board, Nease College Fair, St. John’s Middle School Athletic Association, and as an advocate of the students in transition with the A.S.S.I.S.T. program. She has been coaching students for over ten years. Darla has successfully placed 300+ undergraduate, graduate and transfer students. Her specialty is connecting with the student and their parents to ensure exceptional guidance throughout the process. She will inspire your student to be their personal best represented by their essay, resume and application.

Coaching Services

We offer the following services when meeting with students:

  • Student advising and resume prep beginning as early as 8th grade through in person meetings
  • Selecting and setting up college visits
  • Determine appropriate stretch, target, and safety schools
  • Guidance while completing CommonApp, Coalition Application, and individual university applications
  • Register and send scores for SAT/ACT
  • Assisting in the creation and editing of ZeeMee accounts
  • FAFSA application assistance and review
  • Bright Futures application assistance
  • Honors applications and essays review
  • Scholarship applications and essays review
  • Interview prep
  • College selection guidance - finding the right fit for your student
  • Assisting students with Naviance
  • Assisting and advising students in transition to college
  • Preparing for orientation and completing housing applications
